✨ About NalehCosmetics

Crafted Natural Handmade Cosmetics with Care | Trusted Rebranding & Marketing | Customer Satisfaction First | 100% Reliable Delivery Service

👩‍🔬 Who We Are

NalehCosmetics is a proud Tamil Nadu–based brand dedicated to creating natural handmade cosmetics rooted in Siddha and Ayurveda. We combine traditional herbal wisdom with modern skincare practices, delivering products that are pure, safe, and effective.

Founded in March 2025 by our Creative Head & CEO Kavin J, NalehCosmetics was built on a mission: to offer premium, chemical-free skincare crafted with love, tradition, and authenticity.

Every product is handmade in small batches, ensuring freshness, purity, and personal attention in each jar, bottle, or pack.

💡 What We Do

  • 🌿 Handmade Cosmetics – Crafted with herbs, oils, and Ayurvedic formulas for safe and healthy skincare.
  • 🔄 Rebranding & Marketing – Refining and rebranding select raw-material products into premium-quality offerings.
  • 🤝 Customer-Focused Service – Premium products with premium-level service, delivery, and support.

🤲 Our Promise

  • ✔ Premium Quality – Freshly handcrafted, chemical-free products with guaranteed quality and quantity.
  • ✔ Customer Support 24/7 – Available anytime via call, email, or WhatsApp.
  • ✔ Safe & Reliable Delivery – Track your products until they arrive at your doorstep.
  • ✔ Hassle-Free Returns – Valid issues resolved with easy returns or replacements.

🎯 Our Mission

To promote clean and healthy skincare through natural handmade cosmetics that nourish the skin without compromising on safety, purity, or effectiveness.

🌍 Our Roots

We are proudly located in Kalkulam Taluk, Kanyakumari District, Tamil Nadu, India. As a proprietorship business, we stay grounded in our values—offering products that are gentle on your skin and kind to the earth.

💖 Why Choose NalehCosmetics?

At NalehCosmetics, every product is more than skincare—it’s a promise of purity, care, and tradition. Here’s why our customers trust us:

  • 🌿 Handcrafted with Care – Each product is carefully handmade in small batches, preserving natural richness and ensuring premium quality.
  • 🍃 Made from Nature – Infused with herbs, essential oils, and plant-based ingredients for gentle, effective results.
  • 🚫 No Harsh Chemicals – We never use parabens, sulfates, or synthetic fragrances — only clean, safe, and natural care.
  • 💧 Skin-Loving Formulas – Designed for all skin types with clear product information for confident purchases.
  • 🏡 Locally Produced, Globally Loved – Proudly crafted in Tamil Nadu, shipped with love across India.
