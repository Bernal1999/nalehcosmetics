📦 Return & Replacement Policy

At NalehCosmetics, your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 100% replacement policy for damaged or incorrect items, and a return policy for unused products under specific conditions. Please review our policy below before initiating a request.

🔄 Replacement Policy

  • ✅ 100% Replacement – We will replace your product at no extra cost, including shipping charges.
  • ⏱ Time Limit – Replacement requests must be raised within 2 days of receiving the product.
  • 📷 Proof Required – Photo or unboxing video proof of the damage/defect must be provided.
  • 📦 Process – Once reviewed, a new product will be shipped to you.

↩️ Return Policy

  • ⏱ Time Limit – Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • 📦 Condition – The product must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • 💰 Refund – Approved returns will be refunded for the product cost only.
    • Original shipping charges are non-refundable.
    • Return shipping cost is borne by the customer.
  • 🚚 Process – Customer must arrange return shipping via tracked courier service. NalehCosmetics does not provide pickup service.

📝 How to Request a Return or Replacement

  1. 📞 Contact us via:
    • WhatsApp: +91 83002 99101
    • Email: nalehcosmetics@gmail.com
  2. 📋 Provide your Order ID and product details.
  3. 📷 For damaged/incorrect products, submit photos or an unboxing video as proof.
  4. 📦 Send the product back via tracked courier (for returns).
  5. ✅ Once received and inspected, we’ll issue a replacement or refund (if eligible).

⚠️ Important Notes

  • ❌ Returns or replacements will not be accepted for used products.
  • ⏳ Requests made after the mentioned time limits will not be eligible.
  • 💳 Refunds (if approved) will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days after inspection.

🚚 Shipping Policy

At NalehCosmetics, we aim to deliver your orders safely and on time. Please read our shipping guidelines below:

💰 Shipping Costs & Promotions

  • 🚚 Free Delivery – Enjoy free shipping on all orders ₹500 and above anywhere in India.
  • 📦 Standard Shipping Fee – A small delivery charge applies for orders below ₹500.
  • 🛒 Promotional Offers – From time to time, we may run free shipping or discount promotions. Stay updated via our website or WhatsApp.

📦 Delivery Options

  • Customers can choose their preferred delivery service at checkout.
  • All orders are shipped through reliable courier partners to ensure safe and timely delivery.

⏱ Delivery Time & Tracking

  • 📦 Average Delivery Time – Orders are typically delivered within 7 business days from the date of dispatch.
  • 🔍 Order Tracking – Once your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details via email and WhatsApp on your registered contact information.
  • 📍 Live Tracking – You can track your package in real time until it reaches your doorstep.

⚠️ Important Shipping Notes

  • Delivery timelines may vary depending on your location, courier partner, or unforeseen delays (e.g., holidays, strikes, or natural causes).
  • Orders are processed and shipped Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.
  • Please ensure your shipping address and contact details are correct to avoid delivery issues.
